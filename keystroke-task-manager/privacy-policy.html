Privacy Policy

Keystroke Task Manager — Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Keystroke Task Manager ("the App") handles your data.

The App does not collect any personal information. All data remains exclusively on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or transmit any information

The App operates entirely offline as a local application. We do not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information
  • Any content within the App, such as task titles, project names, or notes
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Crash reports

Data stored locally on your device

The App stores the following data in a SQLite database on your device only:

  • Tasks (title, status, due date, priority, notes, etc.)
  • Projects
  • App settings (theme, timezone, display preferences, etc.)

This data is never transmitted externally and is never accessible to the developer.

3. Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

The App is distributed through Apple Inc.'s App Store. Apple may process your information when you download or purchase the App. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Sponsorship Links

The App may contain optional links to external services that allow you to support the developer. If you choose to use these services, their respective privacy policies apply. The App does not share any data with these services.

4. Your Control Over Data

You have full control over all your data.

  • Storage: Data is stored in the Application Support folder on your macOS device
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes the App itself; data files can be deleted manually from Application Support
  • Backup: You are solely responsible for backing up your data

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.
